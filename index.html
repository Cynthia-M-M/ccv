Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What is AAMECC and what does it do?

A

AAMECC (Africa, Asia, Middle East Chamber of Commerce) is a global organization that fosters business partnerships, trade relations, and policy advocacy across these regions.

Q

How can I become a member of AAMECC?

A

You can become a member by visiting our membership page, selecting the appropriate membership tier, and completing the registration form. Membership options range from individual to corporate.

Q

What are the benefits of joining AAMECC?

A

As a member of AAMECC, you gain access to exclusive networking events, business forums, market intelligence, policy advocacy opportunities, and more, tailored to boost business opportunities across Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

Q

How can I participate in AAMECC events and forums?

A

Visit our events page to view the upcoming conferences, webinars, and business forums. You can register for any event directly from the events page.

Q

Who can I contact for more information?

A

You can contact our support team via the contact form on the contact page or reach us directly at info@aamecc.org or +254712885069 for any inquiries or additional information.

Q

What regions does AAMECC cover?

A

AAMECC primarily focuses on fostering business relationships between Africa, Asia, and the Middle East, creating a powerful network across these three dynamic regions.

Q

What types of businesses can join AAMECC?

A

AAMECC welcomes businesses of all sizes and sectors, from startups to large corporations, particularly those interested in international trade and cross-continental business opportunities.
